Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Elegance

India Avenue takes you on a culinary adventure to the farthest corners of India and allows you to taste the finest in Indian cuisine. The ambience of the regal courts are carefully crafted by the majestic paintings and warm ornaments of that era.

The rich ambience only enriches the flavour of the wonderful array of exotic Indian delicacies brought to your table by our gracious staff. Our glass panelled tandoor kitchen with clay oven enables our patrons to have a rare look into the impeccable creativity behind our chefs' sumptuous dishes.

Fragrant and warm spices from the four corners of the country are delicately blended in meticulous proportions to create the dishes we present to you.
